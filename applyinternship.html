Our Story

Founded in 2010 by tech visionaries Sarah Johnson and Michael Chen, InnovateTech began as a small startup with a big idea: to make cutting-edge technology accessible to businesses of all sizes.

What started as a three-person team working out of a garage has grown into an international technology solutions provider with offices in 8 countries and a diverse team of over 500 professionals.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of innovation, helping organizations transform their operations, engage customers in new ways, and unlock new opportunities for growth.
