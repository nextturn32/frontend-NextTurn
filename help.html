Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot password". Enter your email address associated with your NxtTurn account, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. If you're already logged in, you can change your password from your account settings.

How can I make my profile more visible to recruiters?

To increase your visibility to recruiters, ensure your profile is complete with a professional photo, detailed work experience, skills, and education. Use industry-relevant keywords in your headline and summary. Regularly engage with content on NxtTurn and maintain an active presence. You can also turn on the "Open to Work" feature to signal to recruiters that you're seeking opportunities.

How do I export my connections?

To export your connections, go to your network page and click on "Connections" in the top menu. Then click on the settings icon (gear icon) and select "Export connections". You'll be able to choose the format (CSV or vCard) and receive a file with your connections' information. Note that you can only export basic information that's visible to you.

What's the difference between a personal and company page?

A personal page represents an individual professional and is used for networking, job searching, and personal branding. A company page represents a business or organization and is used for recruiting, marketing, and sharing updates about the company. Company pages are managed by designated administrators who are typically employees of the organization.
